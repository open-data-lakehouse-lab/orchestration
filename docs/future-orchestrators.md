# Future Orchestrators

The current orchestration layer is designed to be simple and local-first. As the project evolves, we may evaluate more feature-rich orchestrators to handle more complex scenarios, such as:

- Distributed execution.
- Complex dependency management.
- Built-in scheduling and monitoring.
- Cloud-native integration.

## Potential Candidates

Some potential orchestrators that could be explored in the future include:

- **Apache Airflow**: The industry standard for workflow management.
- **Dagster**: A data orchestrator for machine learning, analytics, and ETL.
- **Prefect**: A modern workflow orchestration tool.
- **AWS Step Functions / Google Cloud Workflows**: For serverless orchestration in the cloud.

## Selection Strategy

No final choice has been made yet. The selection of a more advanced orchestrator will depend on the project's requirements for scalability, ease of use, and integration with other cloud services. For now, the focus remains on building a solid, independent foundation using simple local tools.
